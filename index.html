Our Story

The businesswoman's name is Maria Joycenyl E. Reyes, and the brand she founded is called MQ Cosmetics. The beauty brand has been making a name for itself thanks to the quality of the products as well as the dedication and personal touch Reyes and her team put into the business.

A journery of resilience and success. founded by Maria Joycenyl E. Reyes, also known as MQ, the brand has quickly gained popularity as a result of MQ's determination and innovative marketing strategies. MQ Cosmetics started as a humble venture during the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic.

MQ CPSMETIC STARTED MQ Cosmetics first made its mark in the industry with the introduction of their signature product, the Macaroon Lip Balm. This unique lip balm, inspired by the delicate and vibrant colors of macarons, quickly gained popularity for its long-lasting formula, moisturizing properties, and wide range of stunning shades. The Macaroon Lip Balm became an instant hit among beauty enthusiasts, setting the stage for MQ Cosmetics’ rapid growth and success.

Outlook

THE Covid-19 pandemic may have caused a lot of pains, especially to those who got ill or lost their loved ones from this dreaded disease, but there are also beautiful stories that came out of this most challenging crisis of the 21st century.

MQ Cosmetics is one of them, with its inspiring tenacity and success to withstand this global health malady—thanks to its founder’s determination and innovative marketing strategies.
